Video Optimization Tools

  • FreeConvert - Free online video compressor
  • VEED.IO - Online video compressor with quality control
  • Online Converter - Simple video optimization

After downloading your video, you may need to compress it to reduce file size for better web performance.

How to Add Your Video

  1. Find and download a suitable video from one of the resources above
  2. Optimize/compress the video if needed
  3. Rename the video to hero-video.mp4
  4. Place the video in the assets/videos/ folder of your website
  5. The video should now appear as your hero background!
