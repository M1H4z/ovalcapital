Company Overview

Oval Group of Companies

The Oval Group of Companies is a Malaysian-based conglomerate specializing in smart infrastructure, construction, agriculture technology, and digital transformation. United by a shared mission of innovation, sustainability, and community impact, the group comprises several entities that work synergistically across multiple industries:

1. Pembinaan Oval Sdn Bhd (POSB)

  • Established: October 31, 2017
  • Ownership: 100% Bumiputera-owned and operated
  • Core Focus: General construction, infrastructure development, smart cities, and digital services
  • Milestone: In 2022, POSB expanded into the telecommunications sector by securing a major subcontract for the construction of telecom towers in Sarawak.

Strategic Vision:

  • Evolve into a leading telecommunication infrastructure and digital services provider.
  • Align with national and state-level strategies such as the Sarawak Digital Economy Blueprint 2030 and PCDS 2030.

Key Areas of Expertise:

  • Smart Homes, Smart Offices, and AI-integrated Traffic Systems
  • Connectivity in remote regions
  • Renewable energy and green technologies
  • Urban mobility and IoT-driven infrastructure

2. Oval Rice Sdn Bhd (ORSB)

  • Focus: Revolutionizing paddy cultivation and rice milling using Agriculture 4.0 principles
  • Mission: Maximize yields, enhance sustainability, and ensure food security through smart farming.

Technological Edge:

  • IoT-enabled soil and environment sensors
  • AI-driven yield predictions and disease detection
  • Drone-assisted monitoring
  • Blockchain-based rice traceability
  • Automated milling with high efficiency and minimal grain loss

3. Oval Nursery Sdn Bhd (ONSB)

  • Purpose: Large-scale, tech-enabled seedling production to support smart paddy farming

Key Practices:

  • Controlled environment greenhouses
  • Automated irrigation and fertilization
  • Drone-based monitoring and analytics
  • Blockchain traceability for seedlings

4. Oval Machinery Solution Sdn Bhd (OMSSB)

  • Specialization: Mechanized farming solutions for paddy cultivation

Services:

  • Design and manufacturing of custom agricultural machinery
  • Rental, testing, and maintenance of farm equipment
  • Mechanization of all farming stages for labor and cost efficiency

Corporate Philosophy & Commitments

  • Innovation: Technology-first approach across all sectors
  • Sustainability: Emphasis on eco-friendly methods and renewable energy
  • Community Impact: Active in CSR through food aid, disaster relief, educational support, and funding of local institutions
