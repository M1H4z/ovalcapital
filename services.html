1. Smart Construction & Infrastructure Development

Pembinaan Oval Sdn Bhd (POSB) is the flagship company of the Oval Group, leading the way in infrastructure development through innovation and precision.

General Construction & Maintenance

Delivering high-quality construction services for residential, commercial, and industrial projects, built on safety, sustainability, and technical excellence.

Building Information Modelling (BIM)

Utilizing 3D BIM to optimize planning, enhance design accuracy, and streamline execution for cost-effective project delivery.

Smart City Infrastructure Development

POSB integrates intelligent technologies into the urban fabric to promote sustainability and livability:

  • Smart Homes & Offices: Equipped with automated lighting, climate control, and energy-efficient systems.
  • Smart Traffic Light Management: AI-based systems to improve traffic flow and reduce congestion.
  • Smart Parking Solutions: Real-time parking availability, automated payments, and user-friendly interfaces.

Infrastructure for Remote Areas

Providing connectivity infrastructure to underserved communities, including telecommunications towers and broadband expansion.

2. Digital Transformation & Technology Integration

Oval Group is dedicated to propelling Malaysia's digital future by aligning with national blueprints and strategic tech advancements.

Digital Solutions for Businesses & SMEs

Empowering businesses with digital tools, cloud solutions, and IT infrastructure to streamline operations and drive innovation.

IoT, AI & Data Analytics Implementation

Deploying intelligent systems to monitor, manage, and optimize infrastructure performance and service delivery.

Smart City and Smart System Development

Focused on urban mobility, environmental sustainability, and public safety through intelligent technologies.

3. Advanced Agricultural Solutions (Agriculture 4.0)

Oval Rice Sdn Bhd (ORSB) and Oval Nursery Sdn Bhd (ONSB) are spearheading a new era in smart farming, especially in paddy cultivation.

Precision Farming with IoT & AI

Soil sensors, weather-linked irrigation, and AI analytics to optimize resource use, enhance yields, and promote sustainable practices.

Drone-Assisted Crop Management

Real-time imaging and AI-powered analytics for crop health monitoring, disease detection, and yield forecasting.

Automated Irrigation & Drainage Systems

Smart systems that adapt water delivery based on soil and weather data, ensuring optimal plant growth and water conservation.

Smart Rice Milling

Automation and blockchain-based traceability systems to ensure product quality, transparency, and energy efficiency.

4. Agricultural Machinery & Mechanization

Oval Machinery Solution Sdn Bhd (OMSSB) delivers modern machinery solutions for efficient, large-scale farming operations.

Farm Machinery Design & Manufacturing

Creating customized equipment for paddy cultivation and harvesting, tailored to local needs and conditions.

Rental & Maintenance Services

Offering access to high-performance agricultural machines without the burden of ownership.

Mechanization for Paddy Cultivation

Enabling full-cycle mechanization—from seedbed preparation to post-harvest handling—to reduce labor dependency and enhance productivity.

5. Renewable Energy & Green Technology

Sustainability is central to Oval Group's mission, reflected in our growing focus on clean technologies.

Renewable Energy Solutions

Developing solar and other renewable energy projects to power smart cities and rural infrastructures.

Green Infrastructure Integration

Incorporating eco-friendly materials and energy-efficient designs in all construction and infrastructure projects.

6. Research, Innovation & Strategic Collaboration

Innovation is embedded in our DNA. We invest in research and foster partnerships to push boundaries in technology and service delivery.

Emerging Tech R&D

Exploring next-gen technologies in construction, telecommunications, agriculture, and energy.

Strategic Partnerships

Collaborating with government agencies, academia, and private enterprises to scale innovations and access new markets.
